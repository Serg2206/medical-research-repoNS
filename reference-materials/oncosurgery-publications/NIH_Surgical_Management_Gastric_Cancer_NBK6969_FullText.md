# Surgical Management of Gastric Cancer

## Key Points
- **Surgical resection** remains the primary curative treatment for gastric cancer (GC).
- **Prognosis**: 5-year survival rate rarely exceeds 20% in population-based series.
- **Adjuvant therapies** (chemotherapy, radiotherapy) show limited efficacy in improving survival.
